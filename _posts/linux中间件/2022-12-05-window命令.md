## 查询被占用端口并kill

```basic
PC> netstat -aon | findstr "${被占用端口}"
PC> kill ${进程id}

# 例
PS C:\Users\Administrator> netstat -aon | findstr "8081"
  TCP    0.0.0.0:8081           0.0.0.0:0              LISTENING       29260
  TCP    [::]:8081              [::]:0                 LISTENING       29260
PS C:\Users\Administrator> kill 29260
```
